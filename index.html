Who Am I

I am a curious problem-solver who enjoys ruminating over interesting technical problems. Ever since I was a kid, I have enjoyed taking things apart to see how they worked. I fully embraced the ethos "move fast and break things" (mostly the breaking part). But now, to make up for past misdeeds, my attention is focused on finding solutions.

My experience spans the disciplines of computer software and electrical engineering. I have built full stack apps using React\Redux, JavaScript, and Ruby on Rails and have used MATLAB to perform research on autonomous vehicles.
